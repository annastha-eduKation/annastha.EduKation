So, I bet all of you reading this passage have most probably gotten an education.
and I can totally relate, I have also gotten an education, and thats probably the
reason why I am writing this website. In fact, its not just you and me who have gotten
an education, but another 1 million children go to school each school day, and learn many new things.
And that huge number, no doubt. But, is it enough? Factually, an estimated 617 million children and adolescents
around the world are unable to reach minimum proficiency levels in reading and mathematics,
even though two thirds of them are in school. And the reasons are they cannot afford to go to school, have to help
their parents at home to keep the family running, etc. Ultimately, I feel this is a very harsh social crisis
that people should need to be aware of. Just think of all of the children, and adolescents that are not being
able to get a good education just because of their financial and social status.
